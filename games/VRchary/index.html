VRchary

Project Description

Futuristic Virtual Reality archary defense game with heart-rate monitor integration. GameDev@MSU 2017 Capstone Project.

  • Difficulty is regulated by heartrate (lower BPM, more enemies to dodge)
  • Team of 10 students at MSU - 15 week project
  • Received "Best Virtual Reality Game" Award
  • Primary Role: Gameplay and Interaction programming
