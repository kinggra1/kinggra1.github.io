Xibalba

Project Description

An action puzzle game themed around the story of the Mayan sun god and his journey to retrieve his father's soul from Xibalba, the Mayan Underworld. The player takes the role of the sun god as he travels through a series of themed "houses" in Xibalba in a 2.5D perspective.

  • Thematic Puzzle Adventure Game
  • Team of 6 students at MSU - 15 week project
  • Primary Role: Character and Systems Programmer
